Simplify event promotion with Planaby Widgets on your website. Post once to planaby.com and events are instantly listed in the Planaby mobile app, your own website and unlimited other places, all at once.

== Description ==

Planaby puts plans in hands. 

Do more with your events than simply list them on your Wordpress Site. With one post at Planaby.com your events will be discoverable with the Planaby App, on planaby.com and easily listed on yours, or other's websites by placing the Planaby Widget into the footer, Sidebar, or other location on any website.

You can even customize each widget with a different Planaby username so that you can list events from multiple venues.

For step-by-step installation instructions, visit:  https://www.planaby.com/for-plugin-users/

== Installation ==

1. Install the Planaby Shows and Events Widget
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress and locate your account for posting.
3. Drag sidebar widget and configure for your blog, or encourage your fans to install your events on their blog!

You may optionally create an account on Planaby if you want to post new events visit:  http://planaby.com/signup to create your account online.
